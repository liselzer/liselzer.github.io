Surfer Game

2024

Welcome to Surfing Adventure, a fun and interactive game where you ride the waves and dodge obstacles in the ocean. Choose your surfer and test your skills in this exciting challenge!

Project Details

  • Surfers: Choose between a boy surfer and a girl surfer.
  • Obstacles: Avoid shark fins and rocks.
  • Goals: Survive for a set time to win the game.

Techniques & Tools

  • CSS, HTML, and Java web design
  • p5.js development for the game
  • p5.js audio library

Challenges

Balancing the difficulty level was a challenge in itself, as it was important to provide a challenging yet enjoyable experience for players of all skill levels. Incorporating sound effects and background music added to the immersive experience of surfing. Thorough testing was conducted to identify and fix bugs before launch, and gathering user feedback was instrumental in improving gameplay and enhancing the overall user experience.
