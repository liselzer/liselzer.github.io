About Me

Hi! I'm Jane Doe, a surfer by day, aspiring Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) researcher by... well, also by day! When I'm not chasing the perfect wave or getting photo-bombed by Edward Cullen (see above), I'm busy exploring the intersection of creativity and technology.

Whether I'm coding a new app or pretending I’m part of the "Twilight" saga in a cardboard cutout, I aim to blend fun with function. My journey through software development has been one wild ride, much like my surfing adventures — sometimes smooth, sometimes wiping out, but always exciting.

Want to see me in action? Check out this video of me surfing (yes, I do more than just code in front of a screen!). It’s all about balance, both on the waves and in life — and that's what drives my approach to designing intuitive, engaging user experiences.

If you're as excited about HCI, surfing, or slightly embarrassing cutout photos as I am, feel free to explore my projects or reach out. Let's catch some waves — or code — together!
