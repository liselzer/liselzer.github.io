Logo Design

Project Details

  • Design Focus: The "O" in the logo is stylized as a wave to represent fluidity and movement.
  • Tools Used: Designed exclusively with Procreate to ensure detailed and vibrant visuals.
  • Style: The wave is integrated seamlessly into the typography, enhancing the overall aesthetic of the logo.

Techniques & Tools

  • Procreate for digital illustration and design
  • Use of brushes and textures to create a realistic wave effect
  • Vectorization for scalability and adaptability

Challenges

Achieving the right balance between the wave and the letterform was a key challenge. It was crucial to ensure that the wave element was both visually appealing and harmonious with the typography. Iterative design and feedback played a significant role in refining the logo to its final form. Additionally, optimizing the design for various applications, from small digital icons to large print formats, required careful consideration and adjustment.
