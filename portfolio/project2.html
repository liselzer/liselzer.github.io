BioPacific Laboratory Information System

2024

A Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) is specialized software developed to efficiently handle the substantial amounts of data generated during experimental processes. At its core, the LIMS functions as a tracking tool that oversees the journey of a sample throughout its different phases of production and analysis.

Project Details

The project was developed using Python and specialized neuroimaging software to process brain scans. The MIDI notes were then imported into a digital audio workstation (DAW) for further refinement. This work explores the intersection of neuroscience, technology, and art, aiming to create a tangible connection between brain function and creative expression.

Techniques & Tools

  • CSS, HTML, Python web design
  • Javascript testing and maintanence
  • Digital Audio Workstation (DAW)
  • Sound design and music composition

Challenges

The primary challenge was ensuring that the musical notes generated were both accurate and aesthetically pleasing, despite the randomness of brainwaves. Another difficulty was refining the raw data from fMRI scans to make them compatible with musical frameworks.
