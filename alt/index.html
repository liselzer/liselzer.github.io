Wanderer Lost

The Journey

"Wanderer Lost, gentle ocean, stones turned." This narrative is meant to engage the user in a trip through the ocean as a surfer. I orginally wanted to make a journey through the forest but the interactive elements of nature that I wanted to implement for that story would be beyond the capacity of p5.js. So I decided to alter my storyline and make a game of a surfer who is surfing at sunset who must avoid rocks and sharks as he surfs along the wave. I am also going to add a kelp forest instead of a tree forest at the footer-bottom to stay in keeping with my original idea although it has now changed.

Elements of the Journey

  • Gentle stream
  • Skipping stones
  • Lost wanderer
