Surfing Adventure: "Wander lost, Rocks unturned, Sharks biting"

Game Overview

Welcome to Surfing Adventure, you are stuck out at sea and must surf all the way in as rocks and sharks swarm aorund you. Choose your surfer and test your skills in this exciting challenge!

Game Structure

  • Surfers: Choose between a boy surfer and a girl surfer.
  • Obstacles: Avoid shark fins and rocks.
  • Goals: Survive for a set time to win the game.

Visual Elements

The game features waves, rocks, and sharks that the surfer must avoid.

Game Instructions

Use the arrow keys to navigate through the waves.

Click on the surfer you want to use.

Try to avoid obstacles while surfing!
